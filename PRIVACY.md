# Privacy Policy

`compact-comments` does **not** collect, store, or transmit any personal data.

- It runs **entirely locally** in your Claude Code / Claude environment.
- It only reads and rewrites the comments in files you explicitly point it at.
- It makes **no network requests** and sends **no telemetry** — nothing leaves your machine.
- It has no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

Because it processes everything on-device, there is no collected data to access, share, or delete.
